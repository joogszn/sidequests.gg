Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

SideQuests+ ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

When you use SideQuests+, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information: Email address and display name when you create an account via Apple Sign In, Google Sign In, or email.
  • Usage data: Quests created, XP earned, streaks, levels, and other in-app activity to personalize your experience.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting and improvement.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and personalize the SideQuests+ experience
  • To sync your progress across devices
  • To send optional notifications and reminders you have configured
  • To improve the app based on aggregate, anonymized usage patterns

AI Coach

SideQuests+ includes an optional AI-powered coaching feature. When you interact with the AI Coach, your messages and relevant context (such as current quests and goals) are processed to generate personalized responses. We do not sell or share this data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, with industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. We use Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure users can only access their own data.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Authentication and database hosting
  • Apple: Sign In with Apple authentication
  • Google: Google Sign In authentication
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of optional features like the AI Coach
  • Export your data upon request

Children's Privacy

SideQuests+ is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or by email.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@sidequests.gg.
